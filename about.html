What is BPA?

Business Professionals of America is a Career and Technical Student Organization for students pursuing careers in business management, finance, technology, office administration, and related fields.

At Lebanon Trail, members compete, lead chapter operations, organize service initiatives, and gain experience that translates directly into professional settings.
